About

Hello Everyone!

My name is Joanna and I am taking a part in the application process to CodeYourFuture. I have decided to give myself a chance to become a Software Developer, because I am happy to create new things, make stuff working and understand how it works.

I have built this page with the motive of baking. This is, because my other passion is baking! :) I am very good in this and also I can bake gluten, dairy and egg-free cakes, which is very difficult if you want to do it "all-in-one".
My expression is: baking is alike programming. You have to understand it, try & fail, until you achieve something, it will make you happy, proud and you can show it off too!
